Age-Appropriate Programs

Carefully designed curricula that match developmental stages and learning abilities.

Mini Explorers

Ages 3-6

Foundation building through play-based learning, introducing basic concepts through stories, colors, and hands-on activities.

  • Number recognition and counting
  • Shape and pattern exploration
  • Spatial reasoning through play
  • Mathematical storytelling

Junior Thinkers

  • Geometric construction
  • Mathematical reasoning
  • Real-world applications

Advanced mathematical thinking through challenging problems, visual proofs, and competitive mathematics.
