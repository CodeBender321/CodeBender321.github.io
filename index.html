Our Programs

Pick Your Adventure 🚀

Three thoughtfully designed programs, each perfectly matched to your child's developmental stage and curiosity level.

🌈
Mini Explorers
Ages 3 – 6

Foundation building through magical play! We introduce numbers, shapes, and patterns through colour, story, and hands-on discovery.

  • 🔢 Number recognition & counting
  • 🔷 Shapes & pattern exploration
  • 🧩 Spatial reasoning through play
  • 📖 Mathematical storytelling
  • 🎨 Color-based learning activities
🔭
Junior Thinkers
Ages 7 – 10

Developing logical minds through puzzles, games, and hands-on experiments. Abstract ideas become concrete, exciting challenges.

  • 💡 Problem-solving strategies
  • 📐 Geometric construction
  • 🧠 Mathematical reasoning
  • 🌍 Real-world applications
  • 🎮 Gamified challenges & quests
🛸
Senior Voyagers
Ages 11 – 16

Advanced concepts brought to life with technology, data, and real-world problem solving. Prepare for academic excellence.

  • 📊 Algebra & data literacy
  • ⚙️ Computational thinking
  • 🔬 STEM project-based learning
  • 📈 Statistics & probability
  • 🏆 Competition prep & Olympiad
