About the Camp

Hands-on, playful math programs for young learners. Focus on patterns, shapes, numbers, logic & curiosity. Learning through stories, games, art, and exploration.

"Explore patterns, shapes, logic & more – through visuals, discovery and play!"

Because math is all about understanding – not memorization!

Age Groups

  • Mini Explorers (Ages 3–5) – KinderTots: Discovery through play, colors, shapes, and number stories.
  • Junior Thinkers (Ages 6–8) – 1st to 3rd Grade: Puzzles, logic games, hands-on math experiments.
  • Senior Champs (Ages 9–12) – 4th to 5th Grade: Real-world math challenges, visual proofs, and fun competitions.
